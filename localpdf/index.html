The Problem We See

Every AI PDF tool today requires you to upload your documents to corporate servers. Contracts, medical records, financial statements, confidential reports - all sent to the cloud, stored indefinitely, and potentially used to train AI models.

This is not just a privacy concern. It is a professional liability. Your documents should stay under your control.

02

What We Believe

Documents Are Sensitive

Your contracts, reports, and records deserve the same protection as your most confidential conversations.

AI Should Be Accessible

Powerful document analysis tools should not cost $30-100/month. Everyone deserves access to AI capabilities.

Local-First is Possible

Modern computers are powerful enough to run sophisticated AI document analysis locally.

Open Source is Trust
