01

The Problem We See

Every AI writing tool today sends your words to corporate servers. Your drafts, your ideas, your private thoughts - all uploaded, stored, and potentially used to train AI models. Writers, journalists, and professionals are forced to choose between AI assistance and privacy.

This isn't just inconvenient. It's a fundamental breach of creative privacy. Your words should belong to you.

02

What We Believe

Writing is Personal

Your drafts, notes, and ideas deserve the same privacy as your spoken conversations. AI assistance shouldn't require surveillance.

AI Should Be Accessible

Powerful writing tools shouldn't cost $20-50/month. Students, indie authors, and small businesses deserve the same AI capabilities as big corporations.

Local-First is Possible

Modern devices are powerful enough to run sophisticated AI locally. Cloud dependency is a business model, not a technical necessity.

Open Source is Trust

You shouldn't have to take our word for it. Every line of code is public. Audit it. Verify our privacy claims yourself.
