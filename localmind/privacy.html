Last Updated: January 18, 2026

Your transcriptions and audio files never leave your device. LocalMind is designed from the ground up to process everything locally. We have zero access to your data.

1. Desktop Application - Zero Data Collection

The LocalMind desktop application:

  • Processes 100% locally: All audio transcription and quality analysis happens on your device
  • No telemetry: We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, or any form of analytics from the app
  • No network calls: After the initial AI model download, the app works completely offline
  • No account required: You don't need to create an account or provide any personal information to use LocalMind
  • Your files stay yours: All transcriptions, reports, and exports are stored locally on your device

2. Website Analytics (Privacy-Friendly)

This website (localmind.github.io) uses Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics service:

  • No cookies: Plausible does not use cookies or track you across websites
  • No personal data: We cannot identify individual visitors
  • GDPR compliant: All data is anonymized and aggregated
  • Transparent: You can view our public analytics dashboard (if we make it public)

We collect basic anonymous metrics like page views and referral sources to understand how people discover LocalMind. Nothing personally identifiable.

3. Email Newsletter (Opt-In Only)

If you choose to provide your email address to receive updates:

  • Voluntary only: Email collection is 100% opt-in. You can skip and download directly.
  • Stored securely: Emails are stored via Formspree, a privacy-focused form service
  • Limited use: We only use your email to notify you of major updates, new features (like SRT export), and security fixes
  • No selling: We will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties
  • Easy unsubscribe: Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link
  • No spam: We send emails sparingly - only for significant updates

4. Feature Survey Data

When you opt-in to the email newsletter, you can optionally check which features you're interested in (e.g., SRT export, real-time transcription):

  • Purpose: Helps us prioritize development based on user demand
  • Anonymous aggregation: We analyze trends (e.g., "80% want SRT export") not individual responses
  • No profiling: We don't build user profiles or track behavior
