Core Features

Professional Transcription Made Private

Everything you need for audio transcription, running entirely on your device.

Real-time Transcription

State-of-the-art Whisper AI speech recognition that handles accents, background noise, and multiple languages with remarkable accuracy.

Speaker Identification

Automatically identifies who's speaking in multi-person recordings. Perfect for meetings, interviews, podcasts, and depositions.

AI Quality Scoring

Chain-of-Thought reasoning provides 20-30% better analysis accuracy. Evaluate customer service quality, compliance, and conversation metrics.

Flexible Export

Export to PDF reports, JSON for developers, or plain text. Your data, your format. No vendor lock-in.

99+ Languages

Transcribe audio in virtually any language. Interface available in 8 languages including English, Hindi, Spanish, Arabic, Russian, Japanese, French, and Chinese.

100% Offline

After initial model download, works completely offline. Process audio on airplanes, in secure facilities, or anywhere without internet.
