01

The Problem We See

The AI revolution is happening, but it's happening on someone else's terms. Every major AI service requires you to upload your data to corporate servers. Your voice recordings, your documents, your most private content - stored, analyzed, and monetized by companies whose interests don't align with yours.

02

Our Solution

We build AI tools that run entirely on your device. No cloud. No accounts. No data collection. Just powerful, open-source software that respects your privacy and works offline. When you use our products, your data stays exactly where it belongs - with you.

Our Commitment
