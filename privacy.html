Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

The Short Version

We don't collect your data. Our products run 100% locally on your device. Your files, your text, your audio - none of it ever touches our servers because we don't have servers to touch. That's not just a feature, it's our founding principle.

Our Philosophy

At Svetozar Technologies, we believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a feature to be sold. We build AI tools that work entirely on your device because we believe that's how software should work.

What We Don't Collect

Let's be clear about what we never collect:

  • Your files and content: Documents, audio files, PDFs, and text you process with our tools never leave your device.
  • Usage data: We don't track how often you use our apps, what features you use, or how you use them.
  • Personal information: We don't require accounts, so we don't have your email, name, or any other personal data.
  • Analytics and telemetry: Our apps don't phone home. Period.
  • Error reports: If something crashes, it stays on your machine. We don't collect crash reports.

How Our Products Work

All Svetozar products are designed to function entirely offline:

  • LocalMind: Audio transcription happens entirely on your device using locally-installed AI models.
  • LocalWrite: Writing enhancement is processed by AI models running on your computer.
  • LocalPDF: PDF processing uses local algorithms with no cloud involvement.

After initial download, our applications work without any internet connection. The only network requests occur when you choose to check for updates or download new AI models.

Website Privacy

When you visit our website (svetozar-technologies.github.io):

  • We use privacy-respecting analytics (Plausible) that doesn't use cookies or track individuals.
  • We don't use advertising trackers or third-party marketing tools.
  • If you sign up for update notifications, we only store your email address for that purpose.

Email Communications

If you voluntarily provide your email address for product updates:

  • We only send infrequent updates about major product releases.
  • We never share, sell, or rent your email to third parties.
  • Every email includes an unsubscribe link for immediate removal.

Third-Party Services

Our applications don't integrate with third-party services that could compromise your privacy. When you download AI models, those downloads come directly from Hugging Face, a trusted open-source model repository.

Open Source Transparency

All our code is open source and available on GitHub under the MIT license. You can:

  • Audit every line of code to verify our privacy claims.
  • Build the applications yourself from source.
  • Modify the code to meet your specific privacy requirements.

Children's Privacy

Since we don't collect any personal data, there's no specific concern about children's data. Our tools can be safely used by people of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this privacy policy, we'll post the changes here with an updated date. However, our core commitment - that your data stays on your device - will never change.

Contact

Questions about our privacy practices? Open an issue on our GitHub repositories or join our discussions:

  • GitHub Organization
  • Community Discussions

Our Promise

We built Svetozar Technologies because we were tired of AI tools that treat user data as a product. Your privacy isn't negotiable. It's the foundation of everything we build.
