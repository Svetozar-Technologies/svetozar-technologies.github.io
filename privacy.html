Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

The Short Version

We don't collect your data. Every Svetozar Technologies product runs 100% locally on your device. Your files, text, audio, and documents never touch our servers — because we don't have servers. That's not a feature. It's our founding principle.

1. Our Philosophy

At Svetozar Technologies, we believe privacy is a fundamental right, not a premium feature. We build AI tools that work entirely on your device because that's how software should work. No accounts. No cloud processing. No data collection. Ever.

2. Desktop Applications — Zero Data Collection

This policy applies to all Svetozar Technologies desktop applications, including LocalMind (audio transcription), LocalWrite (AI writing assistant), and LocalPDF (PDF intelligence). Every application:

  • Processes 100% locally: All AI processing — transcription, writing, summarization, PDF analysis — happens entirely on your device.
  • Collects zero telemetry: We do not collect usage statistics, crash reports, performance metrics, or any form of analytics from our apps.
  • Makes no network calls: After the initial AI model download, all applications work completely offline.
  • Requires no account: You never need to create an account or provide personal information to use any of our products.
  • Keeps your files local: All outputs — transcriptions, documents, reports, and exports — are stored only on your device.

3. Website Analytics

When you visit our website (svetozar-technologies.github.io), we use Plausible Analytics, a privacy-first analytics service:

  • No cookies: Plausible does not use cookies or track you across websites.
  • No personal data: We cannot identify individual visitors.
  • GDPR compliant: All data is anonymized and aggregated.

We collect only basic anonymous metrics like page views and referral sources to understand how people discover our products. Nothing personally identifiable.

4. No Third-Party Tracking

Our website and applications do NOT use:

  • Google Analytics or any invasive analytics
  • Facebook Pixel or advertising trackers
  • Third-party cookies of any kind
  • Browser fingerprinting techniques
  • Cross-site tracking scripts

5. Email Communications

If you voluntarily provide your email address for product updates:

  • 100% opt-in: Email collection is always voluntary. You can download and use all products without providing any personal information.
  • Stored securely: Emails are handled via Formspree, a privacy-focused form service.
  • Limited use: We only send infrequent notifications about major product releases, new features, and security updates.
  • Never shared: We will never sell, rent, or share your email with third parties.
  • Easy unsubscribe: Every email includes a one-click unsubscribe link.

6. Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with third-party services that could compromise your privacy. The only external dependency is the initial AI model download, which comes directly from Hugging Face, a trusted open-source model repository. After that, everything runs offline.

7. Data Security

Desktop applications: Since all data stays on your device, security is determined by your operating system's built-in protections (e.g., FileVault on macOS, BitLocker on Windows).

Website: This site is served over HTTPS. Any form submissions are encrypted in transit.

8. Your Rights (GDPR Compliance)

If you've provided your email address, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of any data we hold (limited to your email).
  • Deletion: Request immediate removal from our email list.
  • Portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Correction: Update your email address at any time.

To exercise these rights, contact us at: kaivalya.pandey@gmail.com

9. Open Source Transparency

All Svetozar Technologies products are open source under the MIT license. You can:

  • Audit every line of code to verify our privacy claims across all repositories.
  • Verify network behavior — confirm our applications make no unauthorized network calls.
  • Build from source — compile any application yourself for maximum trust.
  • Modify freely — adapt the code to meet your specific privacy requirements.

Visit our repositories: github.com/Svetozar-Technologies

10. Children's Privacy

Our products are not directed at children under 13. However, since we collect no personal data through our desktop applications, they can be safely used by people of all ages.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy as we release new products or features. Material changes will be announced via:

  • Email notification (if you're subscribed)
  • Prominent notice on this website
  • GitHub repository announcements

Our core commitment — that your data stays on your device — will never change.
